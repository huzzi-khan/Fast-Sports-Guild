Our Mission & History

Founded to encourage student participation in sports, FAST Sports Guild promotes discipline, fitness and teamwork among students. We organize the annual Battle of Batches (BOB) futsal tournament, our flagship event.

Objectives

  • Organize annual BOB (Battle of Batches) futsal tournament
  • Promote inter-university competitions
  • Organize weekly training sessions
  • Develop leadership through sport
